Create Your CV for Free

Create a CV for Free. Make a CV for Free. Our platform V Resume Builder provides a comprehensive service for quickly and efficiently creating professional CVs and resumes. Whether you are just starting or want to improve your existing job documents, the application is here to help.

Main Features:

  • Quality Assessment: The rating system automatically evaluates the completeness and quality of your CV, providing you with useful feedback based on the entered data.
  • Support for Two Languages: Whether you need a CV in English or Serbian, the V Resume Builder platform allows you to create a document in both languages with equal ease.
  • Document Formats: Choose between DOC and PDF formats for downloading your CV, adapted to labor market standards.
  • Detailed Personal Information: Easily add and edit all personal details necessary for presenting to potential employers.
  • Experience: Highlight your work experience, emphasizing key projects and positions you have held in your career.
  • Education: Provide a detailed account of your education, including academic and professional qualifications.
  • Projects: Share the projects you have worked on, demonstrating your skills and contributions to team tasks.
